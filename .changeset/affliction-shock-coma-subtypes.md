**Add SHOCK and COMA affliction subtypes**

Adds two long-duration condition subtypes to `AFFLICTION_SUBTYPE`: **Shock** (a
prolonged physiological state of shock lasting hours or days, distinct from the
transient combat-shock states tracked on a Trauma) and **Coma**. Both are
available as affliction subtypes in the item sheet, with localization labels and
abbreviations.

Closes #478.
