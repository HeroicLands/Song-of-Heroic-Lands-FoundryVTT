Fine-sifted sand, scattered or dusted over wet ink to absorb excess moisture and prevent smudging. A scribe carries a modest pouch of it at the writing desk, shaking it over inked lines before blotting and wiping clean. The grains preserve the clarity of script on parchment and paper alike.
