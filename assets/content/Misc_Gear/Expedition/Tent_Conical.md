A linen cone pitched over a single pole lashed at the apex, with staked guys running to the ground. The peaked roof sheds rain well and the open ground-frame allows a small fire within—used by hunters, shepherds, and mounted scouts who camp alone or in pairs. The canvas is oiled against weather and the pole breaks into sections for packing.
