Mandrake, known for its psychoactive and toxic properties, is used both medicinally and for poisoning. It contains tropane alkaloids such as hyoscyamine and scopolamine.

### Symptoms

Symptoms include hallucinations, dilation of pupils, dry mouth, rapid heartbeat, and confusion.

### Progression

Large doses cause delirium, seizures, and death due to respiratory failure.

### Treatment

Emesis induced with mustard seed water, activated charcoal, and calming herbal teas with chamomile or valerian to soothe the nervous system and provide supportive care.
