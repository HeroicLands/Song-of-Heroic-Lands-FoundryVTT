Turophobia is an intense, irrational fear of cheese. People with turophobia may experience a range of symptoms when they think about, see, or are near cheese.

### Impulse
Avoiding cheese-containing foods, restaurants, and grocery stores. Abberance (ABE) increases by 1 from the source of fear.

### Disorder
In addition to trait intensity behaviors: Overwhelming fear or panic at the sight or thought of cheese.
Abberance (ABE) increases by 3 (instead of 1) from the source of fear.

### Physical Symptoms
Rapid heartbeat, sweating, trembling, shortness of breath, nausea, or dizziness.

### Intrusive Thoughts
Persistent and distressing thoughts about cheese being repulsive, harmful, or overwhelming.

### Emotional Distress
Deep feelings of dread and helplessness when encountering cheese.
