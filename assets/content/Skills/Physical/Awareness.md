Awareness measures how attuned a character is to their physical surroundings—the rustle of movement in undergrowth, the glint of metal in shadow, or the faint scent of smoke on the wind. It is most commonly tested in situations where a character might passively notice something unusual, though it can also be actively employed when deliberately scanning for threats or hidden objects. Awareness stands in direct opposition to Stealth, forming the fundamental contest between the hidden and the vigilant. Deficiencies in a particular sense may limit or preclude certain tests, while heightened alertness greatly improves the chance of detecting danger.
