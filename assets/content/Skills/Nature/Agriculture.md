Agriculture encompasses the knowledge of cultivating crops, tending orchards and pastures, and managing woodlands for sustained yield. Practitioners understand the soils, seasons, and plant varieties native to their region, and can assess the quality of both land and harvest. The skill also covers the appraisal of farming equipment and the estimation of crop value at market. From the subsistence garden of a cottar to the broad fields of a yeoman, agriculture is the foundation upon which settled civilization rests.
