Perfumery is the art of creating scented preparations—perfumes, incense, scented soaps, and aromatic oils—from combinations of herbs, flowers, animal essences, and mineral ingredients. Practitioners must understand the properties and interactions of aromatic substances, the techniques of extraction and blending, and how to achieve desired effects of fragrance and longevity. The herbalist's knowledge of raw botanical ingredients directly enhances the perfumer's craft, and fine perfumes command high prices among the wealthy.
