Shipwright is the specialized knowledge of designing and constructing watercraft, from small fishing boats and river barges to large seagoing vessels. The shipwright must understand hull geometry, structural engineering, the properties of different timbers, and the techniques of plank joining, caulking, and rigging that transform raw materials into a seaworthy craft. Major construction projects require both design expertise and the coordination of multiple supporting crafts, making the master shipwright as much a project manager as an artisan.
