Cookery is the practical art of preparing food—transforming raw ingredients into meals that nourish, satisfy, and sometimes delight. A skilled cook understands the properties of different foodstuffs, the timing of heat and seasoning, and how to make the most of whatever provisions are available. The quality of a meal affects not only the health of those who eat it but can influence the mood and social dynamics of a gathering. Truly dreadful cooking may cause illness, while exceptional fare enhances the host's standing and persuasive power.
