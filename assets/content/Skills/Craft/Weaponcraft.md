Weaponcraft is the specialized branch of metalsmithing devoted to the forging of arms—swords, axes, mace-heads, spearpoints, arrowheads, and all manner of bladed and blunt weapons. It demands an intimate understanding of how a weapon's form, balance, and temper affect its performance in combat, knowledge that goes beyond general metalworking. The quality of a finished weapon depends on the smith's skill, the grade of materials, and the quality of the forge and tools available. Exceptional weaponsmiths can produce masterwork arms of superior balance and edge retention that command premium prices.
