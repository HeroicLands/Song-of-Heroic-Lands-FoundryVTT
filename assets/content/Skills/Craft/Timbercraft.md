Timbercraft is the knowledge of forests and woodland management—identifying tree species, assessing timber quality, understanding seasonal growth cycles, and selecting the right wood for specific applications. The skill encompasses practical forestry: felling trees efficiently and safely, processing logs into usable lumber, and managing woodlands for sustainable yield. It serves as the essential foundation for Woodworking and construction projects that rely on timber, and timbercraft expertise is also valuable for navigating and surviving in forested terrain.
