Command is the art of forceful persuasion through authority, intimidation, and sheer presence. Where Charm coaxes, Command compels—demanding obedience or compliance through the weight of one's bearing and voice. It is the tool of officers rallying troops, lords issuing decrees, and interrogators breaking resistance. Targets may resist through willpower or, when facing direct physical intimidation, through their own combat readiness and nerve.
