Summoning is the esoteric discipline of calling upon entities from beyond the physical world—spirits, elemental forces, or other supernatural beings—and compelling or negotiating their service. It is among the most dangerous of the arcane arts, as the summoner must maintain control over entities that may be hostile, capricious, or far more powerful than themselves. The skill requires both strong spiritual resonance and exceptional verbal command to properly articulate the invocations and bindings that keep such forces in check.
