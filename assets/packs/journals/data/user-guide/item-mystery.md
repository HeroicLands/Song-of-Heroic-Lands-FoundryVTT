# What Is a Mystery? {#mystery-overview}

A Mystery represents a mystical tradition or school — a coherent body of
supernatural knowledge that groups related Mystical Abilities together.
Think of a Mystery as a "spell list" or "prayer book" that defines a
collection of powers a practitioner studies. Each Mystery belongs to a
broader mystical framework and provides its adherents with specific
capabilities.

# Where It Appears {#mystery-where}

Mysteries belong to Beings. They appear on the Being sheet's **Mystical**
tab. Mysteries serve as an organizational grouping for Mystical Abilities
and may be linked to specific Domains. A character typically learns
Mystical Abilities from within the Mysteries they have access to.

# Key Properties {#mystery-properties}

- **Name** — the tradition or school of mystical knowledge.
- **Description** — the nature, history, and focus of this Mystery.
- **Associated Abilities** — which Mystical Abilities belong to this
  Mystery.
