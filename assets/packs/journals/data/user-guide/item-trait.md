# What Is a Trait? {#trait-overview}

A Trait represents an innate characteristic of a Being — something fundamental
about who or what they are. Traits cover a wide range: physical attributes like
Strength and Stamina, mental attributes like Intelligence and Will, and other
inherent qualities such as Comeliness, Morality, or racial features. Some traits
are **attributes** (those with an intensity of "attribute"), and these serve as
the foundation for skill calculations throughout the system.

# Where It Appears {#trait-where}

Traits belong to Beings. They appear on the Being sheet's **Profile** tab.
Attribute-type traits are displayed prominently since they form the basis of
skill base formulas — every skill derives its starting value from one or more
attributes. Non-attribute traits represent other innate qualities that may
grant bonuses, unlock abilities, or describe the character.

Traits are typically added from compendium packs when creating a character,
not created from scratch.

# Key Properties {#trait-properties}

- **Intensity** — determines how the trait functions. Traits with intensity
  set to "attribute" are treated as core attributes and feed into skill base
  formulas. Other intensity values represent different kinds of innate qualities.
- **Value** — the numeric score for this trait, representing how strong or
  developed the characteristic is.
- **Abbreviation** — a short code (e.g., "STR", "DEX") used in formulas
  and compact displays.
