# What Is an Affliction? {#affliction-overview}

An Affliction represents an ongoing condition affecting a Being or Cohort —
diseases, poisons, curses, magical maladies, or other persistent effects
that have a course and potentially a cure. Unlike injuries (which are
specific wounds), afflictions model conditions that progress over time,
may be transmitted to others, and require specific treatments to resolve.

# Where It Appears {#affliction-where}

Afflictions belong to Beings and Cohorts. They appear on the Being sheet
and track the condition's progression. Afflictions can be added when a
character is exposed to disease, poisoned, cursed, or otherwise affected
by a lasting condition. They are typically sourced from compendium packs
that define specific diseases and poisons.

# Key Properties {#affliction-properties}

- **Transmission** — how the affliction spreads (contact, airborne,
  ingestion, vector, etc.), relevant when determining exposure risk.
- **Course** — the progression timeline of the affliction, including
  stages, severity changes, and duration.
- **Treatment** — what remedies or actions can cure or alleviate the
  condition (herbs, physician skill, mystical healing, rest, etc.).
- **Severity** — the current intensity of the affliction and its
  mechanical effects on the character.
