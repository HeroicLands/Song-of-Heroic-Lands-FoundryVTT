# What Is a Domain? {#domain-overview}

A Domain represents a sphere of mystical authority — a broad area of
supernatural power that a character can access through their Philosophy.
Domains define the boundaries of what kinds of mystical abilities a
practitioner can learn and use. Examples might include domains of healing,
fire, death, nature, or illusion, depending on the game world's mystical
framework.

# Where It Appears {#domain-where}

Domains belong to Beings and Cohorts. They appear on the Being sheet's
**Mystical** tab. Each Domain is linked to a Philosophy, and only characters
who follow that Philosophy can access its Domains. Domains in turn govern
which Mysteries and Mystical Abilities are available to the character.

# Key Properties {#domain-properties}

- **Name** — the sphere of mystical authority this domain covers.
- **Linked Philosophy** — the belief system that grants access to this
  domain.
- **Description** — the nature and scope of the domain's mystical
  influence.
