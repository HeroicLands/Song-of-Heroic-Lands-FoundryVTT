# What Is a Disposition? {#disposition-overview}

A Disposition represents a Being's attitude or reaction toward another
entity — a person, group, or concept. Dispositions capture how a character
feels about something and can influence social interactions, loyalty, and
behavior. They are particularly useful for tracking NPC reactions and
relationships over the course of a campaign.

# Where It Appears {#disposition-where}

Dispositions belong to Beings. They appear on the Being sheet and represent
the character's standing attitudes. A Being can have multiple dispositions
toward different targets, reflecting complex social relationships.

# Key Properties {#disposition-properties}

- **Target** — who or what the disposition is directed toward.
- **Attitude** — the nature and intensity of the character's feelings
  (e.g., friendly, hostile, fearful, loyal).
- **Description** — additional context about why the character holds
  this disposition.
