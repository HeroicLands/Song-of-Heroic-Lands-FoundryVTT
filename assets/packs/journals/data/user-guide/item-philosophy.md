# What Is a Philosophy? {#philosophy-overview}

A Philosophy represents a belief system, religion, or spiritual tradition
that a character follows. In SoHL, philosophies are the gateway to mystical
capabilities — a character must adhere to a philosophy to access domains
of mystical power and learn mystical abilities. Whether it is the worship
of a deity, devotion to a school of arcane study, or a shamanistic
tradition, the philosophy defines the character's spiritual framework.

# Where It Appears {#philosophy-where}

Philosophies belong to Beings and Cohorts. They appear on the Being sheet's
**Mystical** tab. A Philosophy links to one or more Domains, which in turn
govern which Mysteries and Mystical Abilities the character can access.

# Key Properties {#philosophy-properties}

- **Name** — the belief system or tradition the character follows.
- **Description** — the tenets, practices, and worldview of the philosophy.
- **Linked Domains** — the spheres of mystical authority available to
  adherents of this philosophy.
