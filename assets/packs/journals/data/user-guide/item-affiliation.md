# What Is an Affiliation? {#affiliation-overview}

An Affiliation represents a character's membership in a group, faction,
organization, or social structure within the game world. This could be a
guild, a noble house, a military unit, a religious order, or any other
organized body that the character belongs to. Affiliations help define a
character's social standing and connections.

# Where It Appears {#affiliation-where}

Affiliations belong to Beings and Cohorts, and sometimes to Structures and
Vehicles (representing ownership or allegiance). They appear on the Being
sheet's **Profile** tab alongside traits and other identity information.

# Key Properties {#affiliation-properties}

- **Name** — the organization or group the character belongs to.
- **Rank or Title** — the character's position within the organization.
- **Description** — notes about the nature of the membership and any
  obligations or privileges it confers.
