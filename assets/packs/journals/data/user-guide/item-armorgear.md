# What Is Armor Gear? {#armorgear-overview}

Armor Gear represents a wearable piece of protective equipment — a mail
hauberk, a leather jerkin, a great helm, greaves, or a shield. When worn
by a Being, armor gear provides Protection at the body locations it covers,
reducing the damage from incoming attacks. A single piece of armor can cover
multiple body locations, and multiple pieces of armor can be layered for
additional defense.

# Where It Appears {#armorgear-where}

Armor Gear belongs to Beings. It appears on the Being sheet's **Gear** and
**Combat** tabs. Each Armor Gear item contains nested Protection items that
define exactly which body locations it covers and how much protection it
provides at each one. When armor is equipped, these Protection values are
factored into combat resolution.

Armor Gear items are typically added from compendium packs that define
standard equipment.

# Key Properties {#armorgear-properties}

- **Weight** — how heavy the armor is, contributing to encumbrance.
- **Quality** — the craftsmanship of the armor, which may affect its
  protection value and durability.
- **Equipped Status** — whether the armor is currently being worn.
- **Nested Protection Items** — the individual Protection entries that
  define coverage and protection values at specific body locations.
