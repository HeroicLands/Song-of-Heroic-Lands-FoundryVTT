# What Is a Mystical Device? {#mysticaldevice-overview}

A Mystical Device is a magical item — an object imbued with supernatural
power. This could be an enchanted sword, a wand of fire, an amulet of
protection, a holy relic, or any other item that contains mystical
capabilities beyond its mundane properties. Mystical Devices contain
nested Mystical Ability items that define what powers the device grants
to its wielder.

# Where It Appears {#mysticaldevice-where}

Mystical Devices belong to Beings. They appear on both the **Gear** and
**Mystical** tabs of the Being sheet. Each Mystical Device can contain
one or more nested Mystical Abilities, representing the powers bound into
the object. Using a Mystical Device may or may not require the wielder
to have any mystical training of their own, depending on the device.

# Key Properties {#mysticaldevice-properties}

- **Name** — what the device is called.
- **Weight** — the physical weight of the device, contributing to
  encumbrance.
- **Nested Mystical Abilities** — the supernatural powers contained
  within the device.
- **Description** — the appearance, history, and nature of the device.
